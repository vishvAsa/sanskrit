## Pronunciation
Early vedic pronunciation (and consequently euphonic combinations) were different from that of classical Sanskrit.

Major deviations (details elsewhere):- 

- ai=ए, Ai=ऐ, au=ओ, Au=औ.
- emphasis on the udAtta accent instead of svarita.
